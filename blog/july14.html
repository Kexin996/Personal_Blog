Step into DayTrading

Olivia Thu Jul 14, 2022 9999

...

Today, I finished reading the book "How To Day Trading For A Living". We should treat everything like a trade, and never forget to do reflection after every trade, regardless of whether it is successful or not. I had better begin to do paper trading first, to learn how to operate my account and the "fake" tension. I gonna sign up an account this weekend, and continue to the next book for doing algorithmic trading by Python.

Trading is a comprehensive task, and each aspect is important. If I want to be a trader, there should not be the distinction "I can do it" and "I can't do it"; the only difference should be "I can master that, but I can do this better". Never lie to myself, and never try to expect something.

I should treat any company with the attitude of investing it while thinking of the "why" question.

This weekend, I will study C from SCRATCH...

I guess that these are enough for today?
